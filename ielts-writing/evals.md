# IELTS Writing — Evals

Run these 10 pass/fail checks after every skill execution.

## 批改模式

| # | Check | Pass | Fail |
|---|-------|------|------|
| 1 | **四维评分完整** | TR, CC, LR, GRA all scored (0-9, 0.5 intervals) + overall calculated | Missing dimension or wrong range |
| 2 | **字数检查** | Word count reported. Task 1 < 150 or Task 2 < 250 flagged | Not counted or threshold not flagged |
| 3 | **句子级标注** | Every paragraph has specific sentence-level feedback with original quote | Generic feedback without sentence reference |
| 4 | **改写对比存在** | Rewrite section shows original vs improved with bold highlights | No rewrite or no comparison |
| 5 | **改写后重新评分** | Rewritten version has new scores showing improvement | Scores not recalculated |

## 审题模式

| # | Check | Pass | Fail |
|---|-------|------|------|
| 6 | **题型分类正确** | Task 2 type identified (Opinion/Discussion/Adv-Dis/Problem-Sol/Two-part) | Wrong type or not identified |
| 7 | **所有部分都有回应** | Multi-part questions: every part addressed in outline | Part missed |

## 数据完整性

| # | Check | Pass | Fail |
|---|-------|------|------|
| 8 | **提分优先级** | 3 prioritized fixes ranked by impact | No ranking or only 1 fix |
| 9 | **误差提醒** | AI scoring bias (-0.5) mentioned | Not mentioned |
| 10 | **数据持久化** | Essay + report saved, progress.json updated with correct scores | File not written or scores don't match report |
