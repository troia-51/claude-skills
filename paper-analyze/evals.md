# Paper Analyze — Evals

Run these 10 pass/fail checks after every skill execution.

## 分析完整性

| # | Check | Pass | Fail |
|---|-------|------|------|
| 1 | **有 Thesis** | Core claim/thesis of the paper clearly stated | No thesis or vague summary |
| 2 | **有 Methodology** | Method described (not just "they did experiments") — what, how, dataset | Missing or too vague |
| 3 | **有 Key Results** | Specific numbers/benchmarks/metrics cited from the paper | No quantitative results |
| 4 | **有 Limitations** | At least one limitation or weakness identified | Only praise, no critique |
| 5 | **有 Conclusion** | Clear conclusion wrapping up the analysis | Abrupt ending |

## 图片处理

| # | Check | Pass | Fail |
|---|-------|------|------|
| 6 | **图片有 extract** | Key figures/tables from paper are referenced or extracted | No visual content referenced |
| 7 | **图片有说明** | Each referenced figure has a caption explaining what it shows | Figure mentioned without explanation |

## 参考与链接

| # | Check | Pass | Fail |
|---|-------|------|------|
| 8 | **有论文链接** | arXiv / DOI / publisher link included | No source link |
| 9 | **有相关论文** | At least 1 related paper mentioned or suggested | No connections made |

## 格式

| # | Check | Pass | Fail |
|---|-------|------|------|
| 10 | **语言一致** | Output language matches config (zh or en) | Mixed or wrong language |
